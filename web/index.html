Staying safe online

  • Use a unique password for each important account (email, banking, social media).
  • Turn on multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible.
  • Never paste passwords into chat apps or email; use your vault to copy and paste directly into the site.
  • Always check the address bar before logging in—look for the correct domain name, not just the logo.

These are general tips only. Your threat model may need stricter habits.
